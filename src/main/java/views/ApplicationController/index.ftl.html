Rules: You are able to place 4 ships on your grid,the ships can only be placed vertically or horizontally. The submarine can place under others, but the others cannot. All ships cannot place over the edge.

Introduction:each ship have their captain quarter, the captain quarter have a armor except Minesweeper, it will sunk immediately if the CP is be hit, others will minus 1 armor and Miss.

Radar: You can use radar to detect enemy ships when you sunk one ship successfully. (You can only use it two time in the whole game)

Laser: This is your upgrade weapon for submarine, it can hit under water ships as well.

Move: You can move your fleet one position to N, S, E, W. (Only two time.)

Start game: Player's take turns guessing by calling out the coordinates. The opponent responds with "hit" or "miss" as appropriate. Orange is hit, blue is miss, red is sunk.
